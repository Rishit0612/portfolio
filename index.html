Personal · Campus Utility

Sortd

"Your ultimate college daily companion." Sortd solves the chaotic reality of campus life by providing a live class schedule, expense tracking, Udhaari (IOU) management, and quiz & lab-deadline trackers all in one place.

The Need & Impact:
The demand for this utility is massive on campus. Students constantly struggle with forgotten assignment deadlines, scattered class schedules, and losing track of shared expenses (Udhaari) among friends. Sortd unifies these high-friction pain points into a single, beautifully designed ecosystem.

The Experience & Challenges:
Building Sortd was a masterclass in local-first architecture and background processing. The biggest hurdles were ensuring reliable, timezone-aware push notifications for upcoming classes and deadlines without draining battery life. Additionally, engineering a robust two-way sync for the 'Udhaari' system using Supabase required careful conflict resolution to prevent financial data mismatches between peers. I also dove deep into fl_chart to render highly interactive, smooth expense visualizations that parse heavy data points instantly.

Flutter Supabase Provider fl_chart

Hackathon (Nexora'26) · Live

Veritas

"A lie-detector for the internet." Veritas is a Digital Content Provenance platform built on the Monad blockchain that generates cryptographic fingerprints (SHA-256 + perceptual hashes) to create a decentralized registry for digital truth.

The Need & Impact:
In an era where reality can be faked with a single AI prompt, proving what is actually real is a massive challenge. Veritas solves this by immutably anchoring digital assets on-chain, complete with AI forensics to detect tampering. Our prototype was rock-solid and even topped the public Round 1 leaderboard at Nexora'26!

The Experience & Challenges:
This hackathon was a brutal, beautiful learning curve. We spent 9+ continuous hours just grinding through the architecture, writing Solidity smart contracts, and wrestling with Next.js/Wagmi deployment bugs under immense pressure (including nasty SSR hydration issues). While we nailed the technical execution of combining Web3 immutability with AI hashing, the biggest takeaway was a harsh reality check: building a great product and pitching one are two completely different skills. Clean code is essential, but a powerful story is what sells the vision.

Next.js Solidity Foundry Wagmi/viem Monad
